The Stories bundle turns that raw data into polished content — slides, docs, spreadsheets, and posts — across every format an Amplifier team needs.

And it does it with a roster of specialists, not a single tool.

The complication

No single generalist writer is great at every format at once.

Every output format is its own manual workflow. Slides, docs, spreadsheets, and posts each demand different craft, structure, and audience judgment.

A roster of specialists exists precisely to answer that.

How the team works

Each agent owns one role and one audience.

Instead of one writer faking every voice, agents are organized by role and audience — technical, marketing, executive, community, data, research, strategy, adaptation.

And each of those roles is backed by real depth.

technical-writer
Deep technical docs & architecture guides
marketing-writer
External / community communication
executive-briefer

Adapts content between formats
